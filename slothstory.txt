1. Make the dough
2. Shape the dough into a thin circle 2' across and 1/4" deep
3. Roll the edges so they are 3/4" thick
4. Generously, spread the Beau Jo's marina sauce up to the rolled edges
5. Spread 1# of Beau Jo's cheese mix
6. Bake the pizza until done following Beau Jo's special timing
7. Box the pizza piping hot with honey for the rolled crust.
8. Ask delivery person to deliver the pizza to the specified address.
